About

Raising the regional traditions of Nuevo León, comes Music, the most important and representative festival in northern Mexico. With this festival we will make reference to the customs and roots of our city, its entrepreneurial and progressive personality, its industry, its people, its mountains and the modernity that characterizes it. Inside the park, the main restaurants, clubs, and other outstanding twists will be represented that people will also be able to enjoy. It's about attitude and everyone is invited!!
